Armor Stand with Arms
=====================

This mod is a derivative of two armor stand mods by Stuart Jones
(stujones11): the "3d Armor Stand" mod (mcl_armor_stand) that ships with
Mineclonia and VoxeLibre, and the "3d_armor_stand" mod from the 3d_armor
modpack for Minetest Game.

Modifications for this mod Copyright (C) 2026 nandologia.

Models and textures
--------------------
License: CC BY-SA 3.0 (https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0/)

    models/armor_stand_arms.obj
    models/armor_stand_arms_minetest.obj
    textures/armor_stand_arms_item.png
    textures/armor_stand_arms_shield.png

armor_stand_arms.obj and the item icon are extended versions of Mineclonia's
armor stand model (3d_armor_stand.obj) and inventory icon
(3d_armor_stand_item.png), which are themselves licensed CC BY-SA 3.0 by
Stuart Jones. The arms were added on top of the original art.

armor_stand_arms_minetest.obj is an extended version of the 3d_armor
modpack's armor stand model (also named 3d_armor_stand.obj, also CC BY-SA
3.0). The arms were added on top of the original geometry; the model still
uses 3d_armor_stand's own texture atlas at runtime.

armor_stand_arms_shield.png is an unmodified copy of Mineclonia's shield
icon (mcl_shields:mcl_shield_48.png), also CC BY-SA 3.0. It is shown on
VoxeLibre stands in place of that game's own shield icon, which is drawn at
an angle and looks skewed when displayed flat on the stand's arm.

At runtime the mod also displays the running game's armor-stand body model
(3d_armor_entity.obj) and, on Minetest Game, 3d_armor's textures; those
files are provided by Mineclonia/VoxeLibre or the 3d_armor modpack and are
not redistributed here.
